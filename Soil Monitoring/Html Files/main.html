Soil Monitoring Features

1

Soil Moisture Monitoring

The system continuously measures soil moisture levels to determine when the crop needs irrigation.

2

NPK Level Monitoring

The solution monitors nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium levels in the soil to determine fertility.

3

pH Level Monitoring

Soil pH affects overall health. The system measures the pH level in the soil indicating its alkalinity.

4

Remote Monitoring

Farmers can monitor soil conditions remotely using a mobile app or an admin panel.

5

Real-time Data Collection

Sensors collect data on moisture, temperature, pH levels, and nutrient content in real time.

6

Automated Alerts & Notifications

Alerts and notifications are sent to users when soil parameters deviate from the set threshold.
